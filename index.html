What is Dropshipping?

With dropshipping you need no

  • Inventory
  • Coding/design skills
  • Experience
You NEVER handle or ship anything yourself. Items ship directly to your customer from the supplier. You don't even buy anything until someone buys it from you first, at a profit! You then pocket the profit and scale.
